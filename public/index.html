Management Team

Dr. Venkat K Sadagopann – CEO

Dr. Venkat has two decades of experience at senior levels as a healthcare professional both on the clinical as well as on the information technology and services sector. He has wide experience in clinical informatics and product development on the provider vertical. He has domain expertise for building risk stratification tools.

He focuses on overall organizational growth and product strategy and works closely with client organizations to ensure they receive optimal value from Medexos Healthcare.

His key strengths include marketing, sales, product strategy, and business development.

He received his B.D.S. from The Tamilnadu Dr. MGR Medical University, Chennai, and his M.B.A. in Information Technology Management from Schiller International University, Florida, USA
