Payer Services

Risk Adjustment Service

  • Our payer solutions allow clients to improve their risk adjustment and quality programs by giving them a complete platform that gives them actionable data and solutions to act on them
  • Our 360 degree look at the data and in-depth evaluations makes sure that you get the right RAF score and help you maximize the impact

Prospective Risk Review

  • Capture all risk adjusted diagnoses and ensure members are appropriately risk-adjusted
  • We offer a full-service HCC coding solution for all risk adjustment coding: ACA-Commercial, Medicare Advantage, and Managed Medicaid
  • Our compliance-based approach to medical record coding is delivered by AAPC or AHIMA certified coders who capture or suggest deletion of risk adjusting diagnostic codes and can also perform data validation audits

Concurrent Risk Review

  • Provide immediate review of the record
  • Review risk adjustment conditions at time of visit
  • Real-time query for provider clarification

Retrospective Risk Review

  • Reviewing at least a 5 year back dated records
  • Claims data validation
  • Identify opportunities for provider education/training
